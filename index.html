TravisMathew UX Case Study

Redesigning the e-commerce experience

Background

TravisMathew, a premium lifestyle brand, sought to enhance their online presence and improve customer experience.

Problem

Low conversion rates and inconsistent product discoverability.

Solution

Improved navigation, streamlined checkout, and enhanced product visuals.

Key Insights

  • Improved checkout flow reduced cart abandonment by 18%.
  • Enhanced product visuals boosted conversions by 25%.
